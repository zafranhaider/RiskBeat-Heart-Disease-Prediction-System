About Heart Failure Risk

Ejection Fraction

Measures how much blood the left ventricle pumps out with each contraction. Lower values indicate higher risk.

Serum Markers

Elevated creatinine levels indicate kidney issues that can contribute to heart problems.

Lifestyle Factors

Smoking, diabetes, and high blood pressure significantly increase heart failure risk.

Medical Guidance

This tool provides risk assessment only. Consult a cardiologist for diagnosis.
